About Our Farm

Krushnam Organic Farm is nestled in the serene village of Ukadiya, Veraval, Somnath. For over a decade, we've been committed to sustainable farming practices that respect the earth and produce the highest quality organic foods.

Our farm spans over 50 acres of fertile land where we grow a variety of seasonal fruits, vegetables, and grains without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.

Our Farming Philosophy

We believe in working with nature, not against it. Our farming methods enhance soil health, promote biodiversity, and ensure the long-term sustainability of our land while delivering nutritious, flavorful produce to your table.

100% Organic

All our products are certified organic, grown without synthetic chemicals or GMOs.

Sustainable Farming

We practice crop rotation, composting, and water conservation to protect our ecosystem.

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of every product that leaves our farm.
