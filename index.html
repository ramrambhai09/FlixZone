OUR STORY

From Our Fields to Your Home

Krushnam Organic Farm, located in Ukadiya village of Veraval Taluka, Somnath, is committed to delivering pure and natural products straight from nature's heart.

For generations, our family has cultivated the rich soil of Gujarat, following traditional farming methods that respect the earth and its natural cycles.

100% Organic

No chemicals, pesticides, or artificial fertilizers used in our farming process.

Sustainable Farming

We practice crop rotation and natural pest control to maintain soil health.

Quality Guarantee

Every product is carefully selected and tested for quality and freshness.
